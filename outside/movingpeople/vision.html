This is the first act of what can be called "the moving people vision", the first seed of a more complicated project that is shaping gradually. So, when the second part of the project is already beginning to get ready, the first part is released.
Movement... Deconstructing the representaion... Destructuring the mimic... Epiphany of content... Changing the point of view... Searching for the message...
Along with the form, also the location and the intention is lost: Madrid's overcrowded city center is mingled with lonely passengers of Amsterdam, with a metro shot from Lisbon and children from a southern Italian town.
Only movement is the espression of life.
