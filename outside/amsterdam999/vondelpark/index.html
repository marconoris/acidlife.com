VONDEL PARK

This is Wonder Park, a permanent point of peace and joy...


...for someone this is the odd cross of Europe, and, maybe, not only of Europe...
I changed suit, wearing off my sweated business robes, and leaning my camera on a white t-shirt.


Every day, no matter when, here it comes the real party, the real union...
...where nobody is invited.
At first I was scared because, unlike Charles Bridge (Prague) or Coven Gardens (London), everybody was smoking, and it was only 5pm! As a typical Italian tourist, I couldn't get used to the idea of being in a free and safe place so quickly!

I stopped for a while listening people playing guitars and bongos. It was like any other quite part of the World. I smoked a few cigarettes, and met people from Croatia, Cuba, Antilles... and a trio from Mongolia.

You can play bongo, even if you forgot it at home...
...or, if you are of another kind, you can get back uptown and have a romantic boat trip.
